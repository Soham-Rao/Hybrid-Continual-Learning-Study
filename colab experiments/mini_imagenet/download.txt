WHAT TO DOWNLOAD AFTER RUNS

From the results_root you choose (e.g., /content/drive/MyDrive/colab_results/mini_imagenet/resnet18):

1) master_results.csv
   - Aggregated metrics across seeds and methods.

2) raw/
   - Per-run logs (*.log)
   - Per-run metrics (*_metrics.csv)

3) figures/
   - summary_*.png (heatmap + curves + metrics)
   - curves_*.png
   - heatmap_*.png
   - avg_accuracy_*.png, forgetting_*.png, backward_transfer_*.png, forward_transfer_*.png
   - pareto_*.png

4) checkpoints/ (optional)
   - Per-task checkpoints for resuming interrupted runs.
